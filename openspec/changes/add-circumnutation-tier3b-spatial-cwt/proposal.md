## Why

PR #9 of the multi-PR circumnutation program (epic #197, roadmap row #9; tracking issue #229) graduates the `spatial_cwt` stub into an implementation module. It is the spatial sibling of PR #5 `temporal_cwt`: input-validating, frozen `attrs` Results, a determinism contract, **no trait emission**. It consumes PR #8's `MidlineResult` (`curvature_px_inv` on the native NON-uniform `arc_length_px` grid), owns the uniform-arc-length resample (the locked PR #8‚Üí#9 handoff), runs a `cgau2` spatial Continuous Wavelet Transform of Œ∫(s), and extracts the dominant spatial-wavelength ridge Œª(s_a) ‚Äî the steady-traveling-wave quantity PR #10 needs for `traveling_wave_residual` and `apex_basal_period_consistency`.

**Scope was revised after a pre-spec empirical investigation.** A documented `research:investigate` on the 6 Nipponbare plate-001 proofread tracks established that the theory ¬ß7.4 premise ‚Äî `L_gz` = peak of the |Œ∫(s)| envelope, `L_c` = basal exponential decay (Meroz 2026 Eq. 5‚Äì6) ‚Äî **does not transfer to top-view tip-trail Œ∫(s)**: real trails span ‚âà3050‚Äì3527 px, the growth zone (~29‚Äì65 px) is ~1 % of the trail, and the |Œ∫| envelope global-argmax lands basal (48‚Äì100 % of span) on every track and every candidate envelope method (Hilbert, cgau2 CWT-power, smoothed-|Œ∫|). Raw Œ∫(s_a) is a roughly steady-amplitude oscillation over the whole trail ‚Äî the QPB steady-traveling-wave signature (¬ß4.7), exactly as theory ¬ß6.5 warned ("the recent trail cannot be inverted‚Ä¶ trail-as-midline holds only past the growth zone"). The ¬ß7.4 definitions originate from Rivi√®re/Averrhoa whole-organ side-view Œ∫(s,t), not multi-period top-view tip-trail data. PR #9 therefore ships the part the data supports (resample + cgau2 scaleogram + Œª_spatial ridge) and **descopes `L_gz`/`L_c` detection** to a tracked follow-up research issue (#230), which blocks the `L_gz`-dependent parts of PR #10 but not `traveling_wave_residual` / `apex_basal_period_consistency`.
